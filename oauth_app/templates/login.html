Welcome to Hoo's Hot and Cold!

Hoo's Hot and Cold is an interactive, location-based game that brings the classic childhood game of hot and cold into the digital age. Our app offers a unique and engaging way to explore and discover new places around grounds!

How It Works:
  1. Submit Locations for Games: Users like you can submit interesting locations as potential game spots. These could be landmarks, parks, art installations, or any place you find intriguing. Each location becomes a new adventure for players to discover.
  2. Start and Continue Games: Once a location is set, you can start a game. The game's objective is to find the hidden location using the clues provided. You can also continue previously started games, making it easy to play at your own pace.
  3. Navigate with the Interactive Marker: As a player, you'll use an interactive marker on a map interface to search for the hidden location. Move the marker around as you hypothesize where the destination might be.
  4. Use Hints to Your Advantage: Feeling lost? Use the "Get Hint" button! It's a crucial tool in the game, letting you know if you're getting 'hotter' (closer to the location) or 'colder' (farther away). These hints are based on your marker's movements in relation to the hidden location, guiding you towards the target.
  5. Discover and Enjoy: Each game is an opportunity to explore and uncover new places. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, Hoo's Hot and Cold promises a fun and thrilling experience as you unravel the mysteries of each location.
