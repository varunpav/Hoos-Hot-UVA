Hot & Cold FAQ

How to Submit a Game:

Embark on your own creative adventure by tapping "Submit a Game". Navigate the map to drop the red pin on your secret spot. Give your hidden treasure a name, leave a crafty clue, and watch eager explorers follow the breadcrumbs to your challenge. Once your masterpiece is complete, cast it into the world with 'create a game' – your quest is now live for thrill-seekers to discover!

How to Play:

Select "Play a Game" and choose your difficulty level to begin the treasure hunt. Drag the red marker across the map and press ‘get hint’ to uncover clues ranging from 'cold' to 'hot', guiding you to the prize. When 'hot' hints appear, victory is near! If your sleuthing skills lead you to the right spot, celebrate with a triumphant 'You found {name of location}!'. Let the games begin!
